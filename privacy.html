Privacy

This site is designed to collect as little information about you as possible beyond what you choose to share.

The tools on this site

The career reflection and the prompt dissection in Test Your Knowledge both run entirely in your own browser. Your answers never leave your device unless you choose to email them to me. Nothing is saved on a server, and I can't see anything you didn't send.

The contact form

The Connect page uses a Google Form. Anything you submit there is collected and processed by Google under their terms, the same as any Google Form.

Email

Emails you send are stored in a Google archive and handled on their terms. I typically don't delete emails. I'd rather be able to pick up where we left off if you reach back out, and I may keep notes to track our conversations over time. I use what you send only to respond and to help you.

Cookies and tracking

I don't use cookies, analytics, trackers, or advertising pixels. I don't measure who visits or where they came from.

Outside services

The site runs on its own. The fonts, styles, and scripts all load from this domain, not a third party. The only outside services involved are the Google Form on the Connect page and your own email app, if you choose to write to me.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub, which serves the pages, with its domain managed through Porkbun's DNS. Like every website, the host keeps standard server logs like your IP address, your browser type, and which page you requested to deliver the page and guard against abuse. Those logs aren't tied to you, and nothing on the page reports your visit back to me.
